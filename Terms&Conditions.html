In addition to buying products in-store, customers will also be able to use the online website to rent a product. Once customers have chosen the desired product and appropriate options, they can choose an allotted rental time for 10 days to suit their needs best. Once the pick-up and drop-off dates are finalized, customers have two options. Upon rental purchase of a product, customers can opt to have the items shipped to their house or they can pick up their outfit at the local store on the appropriate date. Customers can visit the store prior to their rental date at any time, to try on or change the size they picked. Shipping and handling is free as a courtesy to our customers.

The rental products must be given back by the date the customer specified when they ordered. The product must be returned by 10th day, no exceptions. If they fail to return to the product back in time, they will be charged $4.50 daily until we receive the product. The billing information is accessible from the initial order which is how we will be able to bill them.

If the dress is damaged after being rented, customers will have to pay the damage fees which is specified after examining the extent of the damage at the store.

Because our store has inventory of all sizes, in case the size is not the perfect fit, customers can try on other sizes until they find their perfect one which will be free of charge at the physical location. Despite our 100% guarantee of the best products and the great materials used in our products, if customers are unsatisfied with the quality of the product, the money will be refunded.

We also ensure that once products are given back, all products are properly taken care of. All the clothing items are dry-cleaned and all other items are cleaned in order to make sure it is ready for the next customer, free of charge.
