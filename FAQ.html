How long can I keep my dress/suit?


Upon purchase of the product, a time frame will be chosen by the customer dictating how long they can keep their purchase for. Customers will be responsible for returning the dress back either in store at a free cost or shipping back to the store with a minor fee depending on your location. If not returned on the select date, customers will be fined in additional charges until returned.

My dress doesn't fit! What should I do?


Aphro and Co. urges customers to come in-store when looking to purchase their prom dress for a more accurate sizing. Customers who have rented online can send the dress back in exchange for a more appropriate sizing. Shippings costs for size or color exchanges will all be handled by Aphro and Co.

What if the dress is not in my size in store?


Fortunately, Aphro and Co. allows for online purchases to be done in case the desired product is not available in store. If out of stock, customers can opt to send their email in to get notified when our stock is refilled.
